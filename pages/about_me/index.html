Who am I?


I'm Joe, a highschool junior who's interested in computers and programming. I've been coding for half a decade, learning new things all the time. I fell in love with programming in a 6th grade coding class, after learning a dumbed down version of Java with an online learning service called CodeHS, however, my coding roots go deeper than that. In fourth grade I can remember playing around with Scratch, making games and simple projects, however I have no way of recovering these projects.
